At Angelo Ricci, your privacy is one of our highest priorities. We respect your right to control your personal data and we are committed to ensuring full transparency about how your data is handled and deleted upon request.

1. Scope of This Policy

This Data Deletion Policy applies to all personal data collected by Angelo Ricci, whether through:

Social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, etc.);

2. Types of Data Eligible for Deletion

When you submit a deletion request, we will remove all personal information directly associated with you, including but not limited to:

Personal details: name, phone number, email, address, date of birth (if provided).

Purchase history: order records, payment confirmations, and invoices (except those legally required for accounting).

Communication logs: WhatsApp messages, chat history, email exchanges, and form submissions.

Marketing data: subscriptions, cookies, and analytics profiles used for personalized recommendations.

Account data: login credentials or preferences linked to your account (if applicable).

Certain data may need to be retained for legal, tax, or regulatory reasons, such as:

Accounting or invoicing records required under national law.

Transaction details to prevent fraud or abuse.

Data needed to fulfill pending deliveries or customer service requests.

Such data is securely stored and automatically deleted once legal retention periods expire (usually within 5 years maximum).
